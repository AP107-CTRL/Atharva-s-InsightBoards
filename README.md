⚡ Electricity Consumption & Cost Analysis Dashboard

---

🚀 Overview

The **Electricity Analysis Dashboard** is a Power BI project designed for **EXECUTIVE LEVEL** to monitor electricity consumption, billing amounts, and cost distribution across campuses and institutes.

This dashboard depicts electricity consumption across multiple college campuses located in various regions under a single educational organization, providing centralized visibility into energy usage and cost distribution.

It enables management to analyze energy usage trends, identify high-cost contributors, and make data-driven decisions for cost optimization and energy efficiency.

---

📂 Dataset Information

**Columns Used:**

1. Date of Payment
2. Location
3. Campus
4. Sub-Campus
5. State
6. City
7. Area
8. Multiplying Factor
9. Unit Rate
10. Unit Consumption
11. Bill Amount

---

📊 Key Metrics Visualized

📈 Average Unit Rate
💰 Total Bill Amount
⚡ Total Units Consumed
🏫 Cost Contribution by Institute
🏢 Cost Contribution by Campus
📅 Monthly Cost & Consumption Trends

---

🎛️ User Filters Available

The dashboard provides dynamic filtering for:

📆 Month
🏫 Campus
🏢 Institute
📍 City
📌 Area

These filters allow users to drill down and analyze electricity consumption and cost distribution at a granular level.

📊 Dashboard Insights

1. Identification of high electricity consumption campuses
2. Monthly cost fluctuation trends
3. Comparison of unit rate variations
4. Contribution analysis by institute and campus
5. Area-wise and city-wise energy consumption patterns
